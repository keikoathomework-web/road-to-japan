Is Road to Japan good for absolute beginners? ▼
Yes — 100%. Road to Japan was designed for people who know zero Japanese. You start with the very first character (あ) and build from there. No prior knowledge needed at all.
How much time do I need per day? ▼
Just 10–15 minutes a day is enough to make solid progress. Our daily quests are designed to fit into a lunch break or your morning coffee. Consistency beats marathon sessions every time.

Most of our learners do — when they complete the daily Hiragana quests consistently. Some take 10 days, others 5. The system adapts to your pace using spaced repetition logic.
