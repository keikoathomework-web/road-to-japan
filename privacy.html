Privacy Policy
Last updated: July 1, 2026  ·  Effective immediately
Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

1. Who We Are

Road to Japan is operated by Keiko Sato / Rose Enterprise, based in Japan. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support.roadtojapan@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

Data Type What We Collect Why
Account Email address, name, password (hashed) To create and manage your account
Learning Progress XP, streak, lesson completion, character mastery To track and sync your progress across devices
Payment Billing name, email, subscription status To process and manage your subscription
Usage Pages visited, features used, device type To improve the Service

We never store your card number. Payment card details are processed directly by Stripe and never transmitted to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To sync your learning progress across devices
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To send important account notifications (e.g. receipts, password resets)
  • To improve and personalize your experience
  • To respond to your support requests

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Road to Japan:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication (data stored in secure cloud servers)
  • Stripe — Payment processing (subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Google — Optional sign-in via Google OAuth

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

Road to Japan uses browser localStorage to store your learning progress and preferences locally on your device. This data is not a cookie and is not sent to any server unless you are signed in, in which case it is synced to your account.

We may use cookies for authentication sessions. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect functionality.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Portability — Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out — Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support.roadtojapan@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Road to Japan is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in servers located outside your country (including the United States and Europe) via our third-party service providers. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

10. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords, and row-level security on our database. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a notice on the Service. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
